Photography

I consider photography as a tool that allows me to show the world trough my perspective. Nowadays people pass through the world in their busy lives without noticing what might be hidden in the images that hit their eyes or even taking the time to admire the beauty that surrounds us. What the camera does is that it freezes time and focuses attention on elements that the eye might overlook, providing the author’s point of view to the subject in question.

In brief, my photos represent what I see when I look at the world.

My favorite photos are the ones that are improvised. I don’t like introducing artificial elements to a photo like posing, mise-en-scène or Photoshop, even though these techniques are proven useful in contributing to pass a message to the viewer.

I am not a 'professional' photographer in the sense that I don't intend to earn a living from it. Most of the pictures I have taken are of trips, travels, weddings and other social events or activities.
